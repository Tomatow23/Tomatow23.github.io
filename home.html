About Me

Hello Everybody! I'm Nathan Dunn. I'm a Software Engineer with an Associates in Computer Science - Programming in Java and currently a senior at Northern Michigan University in Computer Science.

I have been interested in web design since I was 14 and started making small websites for fun. Eventually when it was time to go to school I decided to switch paths and focus more on the engineering aspect of programming where I began to compete in competitions and really grow myself as a developer.

This is where I decided computer science was my future. I then began an Internship at GE Healthcare and from there I began to learn a lot about medical equipment but more importantly the way to protect it from cyber attacks and our customers to best protect private information. Alongside this I had to learn how to manage very large amounts of data to accommodate each medical asset in our system due to it's large scale and best optimize our databases for data manipulation with a non-stop stream of new data.

During this time I competed in the ACM programming competition ending at rank 81. This was a huge accomplishment for me as I have never attended an ACM contest prior to that moment. I am currently interested in going into a Software Engineering / Full-Stack Development role. I have experience in all major programming languages through my time at school but I am very familiar with Java and It's Spring framework specifically. I similarly know HTML, CSS and Javascript quite well as well as some of their frameworks.
